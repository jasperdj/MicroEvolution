MicroEvolution: Cell Stage

You are a tiny microorganism trying to survive and evolve in a primordial soup!

Controls:

• Touch/click anywhere to move directly to that location

• Use the joystick in the bottom right for precise movement control

• Tap the + button in bottom left to access evolution upgrades

Basic Gameplay:

• Eat smaller organisms and nutrients (green and blue dots) to gain DNA

• Avoid larger predators (red cells) that can damage or consume you

Upgrades:

• Speed ⚡ - Move faster to catch prey and escape predators

• Size ⭕ - Grow larger to consume bigger organisms

• Efficiency 🧬 - Extract more DNA from consumed matter

• Defense 🛡️ - Toughen your exterior to reduce damage

• Sensing 👁️ - Detect food and predators from further away

Goal: Reach Level 10 to complete the cellular stage!

Game Over

Your microorganism has been consumed by the primordial soup!
